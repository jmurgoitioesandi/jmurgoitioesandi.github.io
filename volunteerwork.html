Engineers for Oversees Development - London branch

I was part of Engineers for Overseas Development from 2017 to 2019. There, I collaborated in the structural design of an Ecolatrines building at the Kumi Hospital in Uganda. This project allowed the hospital a way to combine sanitation facilities such as latrines with the composting of the human waste, which was later used for agriculture.
