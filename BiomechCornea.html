Estimating the biomechanical properties of the cornea and ciliary body

In this study we used the velocity of shear waves propagating through the cornea and ciliary body, measured using ultrasound elasography, to characterize the hyperelastic behavior of the tissues.

  • Zhang, J., Murgoitio-Esandi, J., Qian, X., Li, R., Gong, C., Nankali, A., Hao, L., Xu, B.Y., Oberai, A.A. & Zhou, Q. (2022). High Frequency Ultrasound Elastography to Assess the Nonlinear Elastic Properties of the Cornea and Ciliary Body. IEEE Transactions on Ultrasonics, Ferroelectrics, and Frequency Control.
