Research

See a list of relevant projects below.

Conditional GANs for Inverse Problems

We are using conditional GANs to solve the inverse elasticity problem present when imaging the biomechanical properties of the optic nerve head.

Biomechanical properties of the cornea

We characterize the hyperelastic behavior of the cornea using a simple hyperelastic model and shear wave velocity measurements.

Aqueous humour flow in the eye

We study the correlation between anterior chamber angle-closure and intraocular pressure to help with these prevention of angle-closure glaucoma.

Wave groups impact on a cylinder

We carried out an experimental study where we generated wave groups representing rogue ocean waves and measure the forces that they imposed on a cylinder.

Tsunami bore propagation modelling

We developed a 1D model to quickly estimate tsunami bore progation through buildings by using the Saint-Venant model and a finite-differences scheme.
