Summary

Before deciding that I wanted to dedicate my career to research, I started my professional career as a civil and structural engineer in 2016, as part of the Ports & Marine team of AECOM in London. Three years later, from 2019 to 2020 (the year before to the start of my Ph.D.), I worked for London Marine Consultans, a small company specialized in the design of offshore mooring turrets in the energy sector.

Ports & Marine team of AECOM, London

As part of the Ports & Marine team of AECOM in London, I worked on the design of new marine structures and in the assessment of existing ones. During the three years at AECOM, I mainly worked in the Thames Tideway Tunnel project, which was one of the largest civil engineering projects in the world with a budget over 5 billion dollars. The projects that I would like to highlight are:

  • Marine structures in the Thames Tideway Tunnel project.
  • Assessment of wave overtopping due to vessel generated waves.
